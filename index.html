About TempMails Pro

TempMails Pro is your go‑to free, web‑based disposable email service. No accounts, no passwords, no spam—just a throwaway inbox that lives for 10 minutes. Perfect for one‑time sign‑ups, verifications, and protecting your real email address from unwanted newsletters and trackers.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is TempMails Pro?

A free, receive‑only disposable‑email service. Generate a random address—no signup required—to get verification codes, newsletters, or any one‑time messages, and keep your real inbox clean.

How do I generate a new email address?

Click the Generate New Address button. We’ll display a random email (e.g. user123@ourtempdomain.org) that’s live for 10 minutes.

How long does my temporary inbox last?

Exactly 10 minutes. You’ll see a countdown timer—once it reaches zero, the inbox and all messages are permanently deleted.

Can I pick my domain?

We rotate through several curated domains to maximize availability. You can’t choose one manually— just click “Generate” and use what you get.

Why isn’t my verification email showing up?

  • Double‑check that you copied the correct address.
  • Click Refresh to poll the server again.
  • Some senders block disposable domains—try generating a new address or using a different one.

Can I extend the 10‑minute timer?

Not at this time. To keep the service free and stateless, each session is capped at 10 minutes.

Can I send emails from my TempMails Pro address?

No—it’s a receive‑only service. You can’t reply or send, only receive messages.

Is my data private?

We don’t log personal info or store messages beyond your session. After expiration, everything is permanently deleted.

Do you offer an API?

Yes—our API lets you programmatically generate addresses and fetch messages. Check the “API Documentation” in the footer for details and code samples.

How can I get support or report abuse?

Use the Contact Us link in the footer or email support@tempmailspro.com. We typically reply within 24 hours.
